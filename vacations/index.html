Vacations Dates

Important Information

Please note: All vacation requests must be submitted at least 3 months in advance.

Vacation Rules:

  • Vacation dates are fully booked in the following scenarios:
  • 2 VIP managers request the same week.
    • One Analytical and one Technical team member request the same week.
  • Vacations are not allowed  during   restricted dates  marked on the calendar.
  • Vacations are not allowed:  if   two or more  employees from the same department request the same dates.

Description:

  • Restricted dates (VACATION NOT ALLOWED ).
  • Reserved vacation dates.
  • Approved vacation dates (VIP Department).
  • Approved vacation dates (Technical or Analytics Department).
  • Fully booked (VACATION NOT ALLOWED).
